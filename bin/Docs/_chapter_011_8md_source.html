1 Chapter 1 - Introduction {#chapter1}
2 ==============================================
3 
4 XBRate is a reinforced concrete cross beam load rating tool. Load ratings are performed in accordance with AASHTO Manual for Bridge Evaluation, Load and Resistance
5 Factor Ratings and the Washington State Department of Transportation (WSDOT) Bridge Design Manual.
6 
7 
8 XBRate performs design, legal, and permit level ratings. If necessary, load posting analysis is also performed.
9 
10 
11 XBRate is part of the BridgeLink suite of applications published by WSDOT. XBRate functions as a general purpose, stand alone rating program. It also extends the PGSuper
12 and PGSplice applications to provide integrated cross beam load rating capabilities.
13 
14 ## Grids
15 Input grids are used throughout XBRate. While the contents of each grid is different, the basic commands for ending data into the grids is consistent throughout the program.
16 
17 
18 Two buttons, [+] and [-], are located to the right of each grid. The [+] button is used to add a row to the grid. The [-] button is used to delete the selected rows from the
19 grid.
20 
21 
22 Grid rows may be selected by clicking on the row header in the left-most column.
