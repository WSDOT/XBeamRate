Favorite Reports

Favorite reports help you work more efficiently by reducing the number of reports show in the user interface.

To use favorites, first select your favorite reports by selecting Options > Reporting. Then click on the reports you want to be favorites.

XBRate will then only display Favorites in the View > Reports menu and the menu displayed from the toolbar. You can toggle both of these menus to display only favorites, or to display all reports by clicking the top item on the menu.

Tip: You can create your own Custom Reports and make them your favorites.
