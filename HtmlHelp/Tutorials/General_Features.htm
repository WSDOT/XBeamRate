General Features

XBRate performs load rating analysis for reinforced concrete cross beams. XBRate can model and load rate a cross beam independently from a specific bridge model. The cross beam and pier model is described and the superstructure permanent and live load reactions are input for the rating analysis.

 

XBRate also integrates into PGSuper and PGSplice to provide a direct cross beam load rating analysis for conventional precast-prestressed concrete girder bridges as well as precast-spliced girder bridges. XBRate utilizes the cross beam and pier model defined in these programs, as well as permanent and live load reactions to perform the load rating analysis.

 

This tutorial will focus on the general features of XBRate. The information is applicable to both cases.
